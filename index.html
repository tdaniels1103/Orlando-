💡 Travel Tips

Pro Tip: Purchase park tickets online to save up to 20% and skip the ticket counter lines at Walt Disney World and Universal!
Best Time: Visit theme parks on weekday mornings for the shortest wait times. Arrive 30 minutes before opening.
Getting Around: I-Drive (International Drive) is the heart of Orlando tourism — free shuttle services connect major hotels and attractions.
